Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cost of a slide?

 The slides, regardless of length/model/caliber will be $100 flat, plus shipping.

What generations will you cover?

 Currently we are only offering Gen 1-3 compatable slides

Is it legal to for you to ship me a slide?

 Yes, the Glock slide is just a piece of useless steel, and is not a regulated part defined by the ATF for registration through a FFL dealer

Can I have you do any coatings like Cerakote, Tenifer, or Melonite? Or custom cuts and designs?

 Initially we will be offering bare slides machined from UNS S17400 Stainless Steel, they will be bare and ready for your personal touch. We are working with vendors to keep the cost down on additional offerings such as coatings and additional cuts

Where are your slides sourced and made?

 The S17400 is sourced from China, and the slides are machined in the USA.

Will the slides have a machined surface or will the be bead blasted?

 Our first production batch will be machine finished, we are working with vendors to offer the next batch with a glass bead blast option.
